Input Fields
Monthly Credit Card Volume
"Total amount processed through credit/debit cards each month. "
Monthly Cash Volume
"Total cash payments received monthly. This affects revenue calculations in Cash Discounting and Supplemental Fee modes."
Current Processing Rate
"The effective rate percentage you currently pay for card processing.  Example: Current Processing Rate = Total Fees ÷ Total Monthly Credit Card Volume.  If you process $50,000 monthly in credit/debit cards and pay $1,250 in total fees, your Current Processing rate is 2.50% or  Current Processing Rate = $1,250 ÷ $50,000 ”
Interchange Cost
"The wholesale rate charged by card networks (Visa/Mastercard/Discover/Amex). This is your processor's base cost. Typically, 1.5-2.25%. Use the Interchange Calculator below to get an estimate based on industry type.  See DMP staff for training on how to use this tool."
Price Differential (Dual Pricing)
"Percentage added to card transactions only. Example: 3.5% means a $100 purchase becomes $103.50 for card users."
Menu Markup % (Cash Discounting)
"Percentage added to all displayed prices. Example: 4% markup means a $10 item shows as $10.40 on the menu.  This increase is applied to ALL tenders including card AND cash"
Cash Discount % (Cash Discounting)
"Discount given to cash-paying customers off the marked-up price. Example: 3% discount on a $10.40 menu price = $10.09 final price."
Supplemental Fee %
"Fee charged on ALL transactions, disclosed at checkout, and cannot be waived/removed based on tender type. Example: 3.75% fee on a $100 purchase adds $3.75."


Flat Rate %
"Your new processing rate under the Fee Recuperation Program. Auto-calculated or manually entered.  The auto-calculation is required for Shift4/SkyTab.  For all other providers, this can be manually changed on a case-by-case."
Tax Rate
"Sales tax percentage paid by merchant.  This should be inclusive of ALL sales tax (ie. State, Local, Municipal).
Average Tip % 
"Average tip percentage customers leave over time.”
Business Type
"Restaurant/QSR: Includes tip calculations. Retail: Excludes tips from all calculations."
Calculated Fields
Base Card Volume
"Your actual card sales before tax, tip, and any fees. This is the foundation for all calculations."
Cost Reduction %
"Percentage of current costs eliminated for this category. Over 100% means you're profiting from the program."
Processing Cost Savings
"Monthly amount saved on card processing fees compared to your current rate."
Card Price Increase Collected (Dual Pricing)
"Revenue collected from the price differential on card transactions. Formula: Base Volume × Price Differential %"
Revenue from Cash Differential (Cash Discounting)
"Net revenue from marking up cash prices then giving cash discounts. Can be negative if discount exceeds markup."
Supplemental Fee Collected
"Total fees collected from both card and cash transactions at your set percentage."
Processor Charge
"What your processor charges under the new program. Formula: Total Processed × Flat Rate %"
Processing After Fee Collection
"Your net processing cost after collecting fees. Negative means you're profiting."
Total Net Monthly Gain
"Complete monthly benefit including processing savings and additional revenue from fees."
Annual Impact
"Your total yearly savings. Simply your monthly gain × 12."
Special Indicators
Order of Operations
"Shows the exact sequence of how fees, taxes, and tips are calculated for transparency."
Auto (Flat Rate toggle)
"Automatically calculates optimal flat rate. Turn off to enter a custom rate."
View/Hide (Calculation Details)
"Expand to see complete mathematical breakdown of all calculations."
