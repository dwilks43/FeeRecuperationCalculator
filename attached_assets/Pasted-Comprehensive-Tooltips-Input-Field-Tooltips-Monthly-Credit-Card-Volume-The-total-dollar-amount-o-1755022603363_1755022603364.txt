Comprehensive Tooltips
Input Field Tooltips
Monthly Credit Card Volume
"The total dollar amount of credit card transactions processed per month. This is your gross credit card sales volume."

Current Processing Rate (%)
"Your current effective rate for processing credit cards, including all fees. Usually between 2-4%."

Interchange Cost (%)
"The wholesale cost charged by card networks (Visa/Mastercard). Typically 1.8-2.5% depending on card types."

Flat Rate Processing (%)
"DMP's flat processing rate that will be charged on the adjusted volume. This covers all processing costs."

Tax Rate (%)
"Local sales tax percentage that increases transaction totals. Enter 0 if not applicable."

Tip Rate (%)
"Average tip percentage that increases transaction totals. Common in restaurants (15-25%). Enter 0 if not applicable."

Price Differential (%)
"The markup percentage added to cash prices when customers pay with cards. This covers your processing costs."

Live Preview Section Tooltips
Base Volume
"Your actual sales volume before taxes and tips are added. Calculated as: Credit Card Volume ÷ (1 + Tax Rate + Tip Rate)"

Marked Up Volume
"Base volume after applying the price differential. Calculated as: Base Volume × (1 + Price Differential)"

Adjusted Volume
"Final transaction volume after adding back taxes and tips. This is what gets processed through DMP."

Markup Collected
"Total monthly revenue collected from the price differential. Calculated as: Base Volume × Price Differential"

Processing Fees
"Total monthly fees paid to DMP for processing. Calculated as: Adjusted Volume × Flat Rate"

Processing Savings Section Tooltips
Current Processing Cost
"What you currently pay monthly for credit card processing. Calculated as: Credit Card Volume × Current Processing Rate"

New Processing Cost
"Your actual out-of-pocket cost with DMP's dual pricing system. Calculated as: Processing Fees - Markup Collected. Negative amounts mean you earn money from processing."

Monthly Savings
"The difference between your current processing cost and your new processing cost. Calculated as: Current Cost - New Cost"
